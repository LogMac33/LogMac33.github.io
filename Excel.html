Excel

This Excel project involves creating a pivot table with interactive slicers from athlete data. The pivot table is a powerful tool for summarizing and analyzing large datasets, allowing you to quickly identify patterns and trends within the data. By using interactive slicers, you can easily filter and sort the data to focus on specific aspects, such as performance metrics, training sessions, or demographic information.

The interactive nature of the sheet enhances its usability, making it highly effective for both detailed analysis and quick insights. You can explore the various buttons and slicers to see how they dynamically update the pivot table, providing a customized view of the data based on your selections. This functionality is particularly useful for coaches, analysts, and researchers who need to make data-driven decisions and track progress over time.

Feel free to dive into the sheet and experiment with the interactive features to uncover valuable insights from the athlete data.
